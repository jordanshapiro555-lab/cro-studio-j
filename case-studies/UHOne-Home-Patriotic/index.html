Persona Summary

Primary ICP: The Self-Employed Safeguarder

  1. Summary: As an entrepeneurial maverick, the Safeguarder is interested in financial and health hacking to create the optimal coverage package
  2. Goals: Design own path that meets all healthcare needs
  3. Needs: An insurer who gets their needs for flexibility and independence with a broad product portfolio
  4. Pain Points: Higher income limits access to reduced premiums on ACA marketplace

The Approach

I established via research that brands use patriotic marketing for 3 main reasons. To:

  1. Establish Emotional Connection: Patriotism evokes strong emotions, often positive
  2. Demonstrate Shared Values: Aligning brand image to values that prospective customers take pride in encourages favorable opinion of the brand
  3. Stand Out: Patriotic imagery sets brands apart visually as well as emotionally

The 4 main ways brands appeal to patriotic themes are:

  1. Seasonal applications: Expressing patriotism around patriotic events such as July 4th
  2. Messaging patriotic connection: Marrying a brand to patroitism
  3. Relating values: Messaging shared patroitic values
  4. Clearly patriotic visuals: Displaying Imagery that is overtly American

With a focus on evergreen optimization, I chose to pursue messaging patriotism and core American values to better relate to UHOne's patriotic audience

Test Results

My first successful patriotic marketing test used imagery that spoke to traditionally American ideals with blue collar workers.

My second winner experimented with the importance of copy, determining that a powerful headline can by even more impactful than effective imagery
